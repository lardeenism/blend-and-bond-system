================================================================================
                            THE JOURNEY
================================================================================

Year Founded: 2025

Vision:
  Brew connections, one cup at a time. Blend & Bond Café was born from a passion 
  for exceptional coffee and a belief that the best conversations happen over a 
  warm cup. A space where flavors blend and hearts bond.

Mission:
  Every cup we serve is a chance to blend flavors and bond hearts.

Milestones:

  2025 - THE DREAM BEGINS
  Gerald & Desiree unite their vision to create a café that blends flavors and 
  bonds hearts in Calape, Bohol. With a deep love for coffee culture and 
  entrepreneurship, Gerald envisioned a café that goes beyond serving drinks — 
  a space where bonds are formed and communities thrive.

  2025 - DOORS OPEN
  Blend & Bond Café officially opens in Calunasan, Calape, Bohol, welcoming the 
  local community with handcrafted beverages and warm smiles. Desiree brings 
  creativity and warmth to every aspect, curating the perfect menu and designing 
  a cozy atmosphere where every customer feels right at home.

  2026 - GROWING TOGETHER
  Expanding the menu with artisan pastries and seasonal drinks. Adding delivery 
  service to reach more customers beyond the physical location. The artisan pastry 
  collection is inspired by Desiree's passion for baking.

  2026 - GOING DIGITAL
  Launching our online ordering system to provide a seamless café experience from 
  anywhere. Customers can now browse the full menu, place orders for dine-in, 
  take-out, or delivery, and track their orders in real-time.

Core Values:

  1. QUALITY FIRST
     We source only the finest ingredients and brew each cup with precision 
     and care. Every product is carefully selected for excellence.

  2. MADE WITH LOVE
     Every item on our menu is crafted with passion and served with genuine 
     warmth. Our team takes pride in creating memorable experiences.

  3. COMMUNITY BOND
     We believe cafés are where friendships begin and communities grow stronger. 
     Blend & Bond is more than a business—it's a gathering place for the community.

  4. EXCELLENCE
     We strive for excellence in everything — from our beverages to customer 
     service to the overall customer experience.

================================================================================
                           SYSTEM FEATURES
================================================================================

ORDER MANAGEMENT:

Order Types:
  - Dine-in (Eat at the cafe)
  - Take-out (Take away)
  - Delivery (Delivery to address)

Order Status Workflow:
  1. Pending - Order received, awaiting confirmation
  2. Confirmed - Order confirmed and sent to kitchen
  3. Preparing - Kitchen preparing the order
  4. Ready - Order ready for pickup/serving
  5. Served - Order delivered to customer (dine-in)
  6. Out for Delivery - On the way (delivery orders)
  7. Delivered - Successfully delivered
  8. Completed - Order completed
  9. Cancelled - Order cancelled by customer or staff

Payment Methods:
  - Cash
  - Cash on Delivery (COD)

---

USER AUTHENTICATION:
  - Login system with username and password
  - Password encryption (bcrypt hashing)
  - JWT token-based authentication
  - Session management with token storage
  - Auto-logout on session expiry

---

REVIEW SYSTEM:
  - Product ratings (1-5 stars)
  - Product reviews with comments

  - Rating metrics tracking
  - Customer feedback management

NOTIFICATIONS:

STAFF MANAGEMENT:

                        TECHNOLOGY STACK
================================================================================

FRONTEND:
  - Framework: React 19.2.4
  - Language: TypeScript
  - Build Tool: Vite
  - Routing: React Router 7.15.1
  - UI Components: Lucide React Icons
  - Styling: CSS3 with CSS Variables
  - Animation: Framer Motion
  - State Management: Zustand
  - HTTP Client: Axios
  - Toast Notifications: React Hot Toast
  - Charts: Recharts

BACKEND:
  - Runtime: Node.js
  - Framework: Express 5.2.1
  - Database: PostgreSQL
  - Database Client: pg (node-postgres)
  - Authentication: JWT (jsonwebtoken)
  - Password Hashing: bcryptjs
  - File Upload: Multer
  - CORS: cors
  - Cookie Handling: cookie-parser
  - Environment: dotenv

DEPLOYMENT:
  - Frontend: Vercel (Global CDN)
  - Backend: Render (Node.js Service)
  - Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  - Version Control: GitHub

================================================================================
                        DATABASE STRUCTURE
================================================================================

CORE TABLES:

1. USERS
   - id (Primary Key)
   - username (Unique)
   - password (Hashed)
   - full_name
   - email
   - phone
   - role (admin, staff, customer)
   - avatar
   - is_active
   - last_login
   - Timestamps: created_at, updated_at

2. CATEGORIES
   - id (Primary Key)
   - name (Unique)
   - slug (Unique)
   - icon
   - display_order
   - is_active
   - created_at

3. PRODUCTS
   - id (Primary Key)
   - name
   - description
   - base_price
   - category_id (Foreign Key)
   - image_filename
   - stock
   - has_sizes (for beverages with size options)
   - is_available
   - is_featured
   - is_best_seller
   - avg_rating
   - total_reviews
   - Timestamps: created_at, updated_at

4. PRODUCT_SIZES
   - id (Primary Key)
   - product_id (Foreign Key)
   - size_label (Small, Medium, Large)
   - volume_ml
   - price

5. ORDERS
   - id (Primary Key)
   - order_number (Unique)
   - customer_name
   - phone
   - email
   - order_type (dine-in, take-out, delivery)
   - order_status (workflow statuses)
   - total_price
   - delivery_fee
   - special_instructions
   - Timestamps: created_at, updated_at

6. ORDER_ITEMS
   - id (Primary Key)
   - order_id (Foreign Key)
   - product_id (Foreign Key)
   - quantity
   - price
   - size (if applicable)

7. REVIEWS & RATINGS
   - Product reviews with ratings
   - Overall order reviews
   - Metrics tracking

8. SETTINGS
   - System configuration key-value pairs
   - Types: string, number, boolean, json

9. NOTIFICATIONS
   - Notification logs
   - Types: order events, system alerts
   - User notification tracking
